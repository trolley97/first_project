About

Abu Kaisar Mohammad Masum completed his B.Sc. from Daffodil International University (DIU) in Bangladesh. Now he is a Lecturer in the Dept. of CSE, DIU. Previously he worked as Research Assistant(RA) in Apurba-DIU Research & Development Lab. He has a number of articles published in several Scopus Indexed proceedings of IEEE, Springer, and Elsevier, and has served on several International conferences and journals as a reviewer. Broadly, his methodological research focuses on the Application of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, and Data mining. He currently works on different areas of NLP and Adaptive algorithms.

Mr. Masum has received the Best Researcher Award-2021 organized by DIU and awarded as Best Performed Faculty Member of DIU. He also received the ‘In Recognition of Scholar Publication in Reputed Indexed Journal’ award for the fourth time in the year 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022 by DIU. Mr. Masum is a Supervisor of the DIU - NLP and Machine Learning Research LAB.
